## What is Trellis 3?

Trellis 3 is a web-based AI generation site for creators who want to move from prompts or references into polished visual outputs. It is designed around practical image and video creation workflows for marketing visuals, social posts, concept art, product scenes, and fast creative iteration.

## Why use Trellis 3?

Trellis 3 gives creators a direct browser workflow instead of forcing every idea through heavy editing software. Users can describe a visual direction, generate results, compare options, and continue refining until the output is ready for a campaign, presentation, or creative draft.

## How Trellis 3 works

1. Open Trellis 3 and start with a text prompt or reference material.
2. Choose the generation workflow that matches the visual result you need.
3. Review, refine, and export the generated image or video asset.
